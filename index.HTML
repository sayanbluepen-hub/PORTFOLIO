About Me

Hardware
Meets Ambition

I'm a second-year Electronics & Communication Engineering student at SRM AP University, Kurnool, deeply passionate about how silicon, signals, and software intersect to solve real-world problems.

From building self-balancing robots with PID control to simulating RLC circuits in LTSpice, I approach engineering with both theoretical rigour and hands-on grit. I believe the future belongs to builders who understand hardware at its core.

Long-term, I want to contribute to India's space and defence ecosystem, work on cutting-edge chip architecture, and eventually found a deep-tech company that pushes what's possible.
